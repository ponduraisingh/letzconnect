About us

LetzConnect is a new-age digital platform that caters to all learning needs of it’s user - providing organized learning content, social intranet to connect users, virtual classrooms for live interactions, skilling for job opportunities and so on - all of which is driven by LC’s intuitive system that makes specific recommendations based on the user’s interests. While LetzConnect’s user-friendly one-stop-shop platform drives engagement and measures performance, the Webex integration allows for teachers and learners to connect in real time and access high-quality learning videos for asynchronous learning.

LetzConnect’s Key Offerings:

Smartboard

Smartboard offers the user, at a glance - posts, updates, announcements, and content specific to their areas of interest, curated by LetzConnect’s AI engine. This connects also users to LC’s partner network of Content and Job Marketplace and is its primary driver of engagement.

Learning Management System with Proctored Examination

LMS solutions empower organizations to meet education and workplace learning needs. This system allows for the integration, organization and structuring of all content of the institution or corporate, as well as the ability to create assignments, work on collaborative projects and conduct proctored examinations with automatic/assisted grading

Virtual Classroom

Through an integration to the powerful WebEx, Virtual Classroom is an online conferencing tool that offers a rich array of features such as real-time whiteboarding, breakout rooms, and screen-sharing capabilities for teachers and students who want to communicate,collaborate, and interact in a live online teaching session.

Note: A paid Webex account is required for Virtual Classroom module

Analytics & Reporting

Personalized analytics of a student's skills and personality for improvement and career advancement. Improve learnability and employability quotient to be ahead of peers and develop a profile that is unique.

Content & Job Marketplace

Access to millions of content and job opportunities via LetzConnect’s partner network of Content Providers, Internships and Employment Providers. Through technical platform integrations, the content and opportunities is curated and presented on the smartboard, providing the right information to the right user.

How to get started

To use LetzConnect, you just need an active Webex account and a LetzConnect account. The LetzConnect Account can be set up for you by the Administrator of your organization or institution.

To get started, simply visit us at www.letzconnect.com or write to us at sales@letzconnect.com and our sales team will help create an account for you".
